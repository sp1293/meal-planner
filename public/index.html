— A different kind of meal plan —

Mindful eating, returned to everyday life.

Mitabhukta — Sanskrit for one who eats with measure — is built around an old idea: that food, made with care for who you are, is itself a form of medicine.

i.

For your body, specifically.

We adapt meals to your conditions — diabetes, PCOS, thyroid, blood pressure — and to the way your particular body asks to be fed.

ii.

Regional, not generic.

Pongal, thepla, ragi mudde, kosambari. Real dishes from real kitchens — not a softened version of "Indian food."

iii.

Guided by humans.

Certified nutritionists, yoga teachers, and physiotherapists you can book — for when an app isn't enough.
